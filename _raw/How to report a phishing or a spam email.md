# How-To Guide - How to report a phishing e-mail
## Introduction
This is a small guide on how to report a Phishing email - suitable for users and SD employees.
## Step-by-Step Instructions
Have the email you want to report opened in gmail - just don't click any links.
Go to the more part - the three dots on the upper right corner of the email

Click on them and choose the tab report phishing

4. A ticket will be sent to our security team, who will further check the issue and inform you about the case.

# How to report Spam e-mail
## Introduction
This is a small guide on how to report Spam email - suitable for users and SD employees.
## Step-by-Step Instructions
Have the email you want to report opened in Gmail - just don't click any links.
Go to the more part - the three dots on the upper right corner of the email

Click on them and choose **Report spam**

4. The email will then move to your Spam folder.
