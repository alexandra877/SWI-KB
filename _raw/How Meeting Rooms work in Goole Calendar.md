**Tips before diving into the guide:**

Only the Google Workspace admins who are part of the "Workspace" team can add new meeting rooms to the Google calendar;
If you need a new resource, create a ticket to create a ticket in .
Meeting rooms are known as resources;
For events with fewer than 20 guests, the organizer and guests receive an email with new room details;
For more than 20 guests, only the organizer and the event creator receive an email with new room details.

**How can I book a meeting room (resource) for my event?**

**Add a room to your event**
On your computer, open .
Create a new event or open an existing one.
To edit an existing event, click Edit event.
If your account is part of an organization with Rooms, click Rooms.
Based on who you invite, you can choose from suggested rooms or search for a new one.
Click the rooms or resources you want to add.
Click Save.
You can book more than one room for an event.
**Remove a room from your event**
On your computer, open .
Open an existing event.
At the top right, click Edit event
In the "Rooms" list, find the room or resource.
Next to the room or resource, click Remove room
Click Save.
**Why a room declines your event**
Recurring events:
Make sure the room's available for at least half of the events, and isn't unavailable more than 8 times.
To book a room for a recurring event:
At the top of the "Rooms" list, click the drop-down.
Click Rooms for future events.

**How can I subscribe to a resource?**
On your computer, open ;

Click on the abbreviation of the building you are looking for;
Click on the checkbox of the resource.

**How to use Companion Mode?**
**1. Connect to a meeting**
In a meeting invite, click Join.
On the meeting landing page, under Other joining options, click Use Companion mode.
Click Check in to associate your name with a meeting room and display your name in the room’s video tile. The room name is also shown in your personal video tile.
If you don’t check in, other participants can’t see your name in either place.
If there is more than one meeting room, select the room you are in.
To check in to a room after the meeting starts, click the meeting name and select Check in to a room.
**Note:** For full audio and video control, join in non-Companion mode on another screen.
**2. Turn on your camera**
In a video meeting, click Turn on the camera.
When your camera is on:
If you checked in to a meeting room, the room name appears in your personal video tile. Your participant tile also shows up:
When you are presenting.
Under the room name for other Meet users and on the meeting-room hardware.
If you did not check-in,  your participant tiles shows up to other Meet users, but does not show up on meeting-room hardware.
**3. Share your screen**
In the video meeting, click Present now.
Choose to share your entire screen, a window, or a tab.
**Note: **Your participant tile also shows up if you joined in Companion mode and checked in to a meeting room.
