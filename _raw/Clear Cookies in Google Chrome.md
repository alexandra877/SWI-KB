# Clear Cookies in Google Chrome
## Introduction
Applicable both for users and for SD staff

## Step-by-Step Instructions
On your computer, open Chrome.
At the top right, click on the 3 vertical dots and then click on Delete browsing data...
You can also use the the shortcut by pressing Ctrl + Shift + Del on your keyboard.
Select a time range. You should select “All time” so that you can clear your cached cookies.
You can select the items to be removed such as “Cookies and other site data” and “Cached images and files”.
Click Clear data.
