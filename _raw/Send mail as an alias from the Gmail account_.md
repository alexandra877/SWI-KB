| How-to Guide | In your Visma Gmail follow the steps below: Go to Settings Select See all settings Go to section Accounts and Import   Down the page on section Send mail as: click on Add another email address Fill in the alias (address) you want to send mails on behalf of and click on Next In the next step, click Send Verification. You will receive a mail to confirm the verification and after you confirm it, you will be able to use your newly added alias to send mails as. |
