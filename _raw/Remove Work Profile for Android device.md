| How-to Guide | Some of these steps might not be the same on every device. You can only delete your work profile if you own your device. To delete your work profile: Go to Settings > Accounts > Remove work profile. Tap Delete to confirm the removal of all apps and data within your work profile. Ensure that the policy app ("Device Policy") is uninstalled and not present on your device. After the work profile is deleted, all local data on the device within that profile is deleted. If this is not working for you, share with us your serial number or IMEI of the correct device and request a wipe remotely. Please keep in mind that the result of the wipe is depending on your setup: 1. You've set up your private email as primary and the Visma account as secondary email. In this way, when you have an Android, a work profile is created which we can delete and everything in the work profile only. Private data should stay intact. 2. You've set up the Visma account as the primary account on the phone. If we take the action to wipe your account your whole phone will be set to factory settings. All your data will be lost including personal data. In both cases, we advise creating a backup if there is data on it that you want to keep. |
