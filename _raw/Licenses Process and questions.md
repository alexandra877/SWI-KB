Licence request: General process

The general process for a license request is the following:

User requests a license-> they are asked to go to mytools.com.
The request from mytools, will go to their manager for approval, and provisioned
automatically if the specific license provisioning is automated. If the process is not
automated, a Jira ticket will be created on behalf of the user, which will end up in support.
If the license the user is looking for cannot be found in mytools, users need to go to
support.visma.com, and raise a request. This issue will end up in Procurement.

Support FAQs

Q: How do I purchase a GitHub license

A: To receive a GitHub license, you need to create a request at support.visma.com.

Q: Procurement of Microsoft licenses (CSP/NCE, Office 365)

A: To purchase Microsoft (CSP/NCE) licenses, please create a request at
support.visma.com, provide your manager's approval, and our support team will be able to
assist.

Q: I need an Adobe license.

A: To purchase Adobe licenses, please create a request at support.visma.com, provide your
manager's approval, and our support team will be able to assist.

Q: How do I get a Visual Studio license?

A: To purchase a Visual Studio license, please create a request at support.visma.com, write
the Visual Studio license in the title of the request to have it reach the correct team. Also,
provide your manager's approval, and our support team will be able to assist.
They come as a default together with the GitHub Enterprise license.

Q: Docker license.

A: To purchase a Docker license, please create a request at support.visma.com, and the
Tools team will be able to assist.

Q: Browserstack, Figma, JetBrains, Productboard licences.

Q: I need a Power BI Pro license

A: Please go to support.visma.com and submit a ticket there. You should write Power BI Pro
license in the title of the request to have it reach the correct team. The approval from your

Q: How to renew JetBrains licenses?

license in the title of the request to ensure it reaches the correct team. The approval from
your manager is required.

Q: How to get VismaGPT licence?

A: Go to MyTools.visma.com, log in, search VismaGPT in one word, and on the row with text
“Need help with this resource?” click on the downward arrow and click on Access.
This request will go to your manager, and after the approval, the Visma GPT license will be
automatically assigned to you.

Q: How to get a Microsoft Teams licence?

A: To purchase Microsoft Teams licenses, please create a request at support.visma.com,
provide your manager's approval, and our support team will be able to assist.

Q: How to get an Azure subscription?

A: To get any type of Cloud account (AWS, Azure, GCP), follow the steps from here
https://confluence.visma.com/display/CTO/Understanding+Cloud+Account+Request+Ticket
