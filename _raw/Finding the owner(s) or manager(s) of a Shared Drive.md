# Finding the owner(s) or manager(s) of a Shared Drive
## Introduction
This guide will show you how to find the manager(s) of a shared drive you are already part of.

## Step-by-Step Instructions
On your browser, access the shared drive you want to view the members for.
Once you selected the shared drive, on the top left corner, under the name of the shared drive you will see a number followed by people 3 people or 1 group.
This will indicate how many people have access to the drive.
Click on the mentioned text and this will open a window displaying all the members.
The ones that have Manager role will be able to add new members to the shared drive.

If you are not part of the shared drive, you can ask a colleague that is already member to follow the steps above so that the managers can add you.
If you encounter any issues or if a shared drive doesn’t have any managers working in Visma, please create a request at support.visma.com and we will gladly help.

## FAQ
Question: Who can add new members to a shared drive?
Answer: Only the members that have the Manager role to the shared drive.
