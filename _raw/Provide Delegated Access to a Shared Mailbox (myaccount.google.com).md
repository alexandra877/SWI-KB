| Description | Using https://mail.google.com/ to delegate access to a Shared Mailbox |
| --- | --- |
| Purpose / Audience | Helping anyone in Visma that is an owner of a Service Account / Shared Mailbox to delegate access |
| Scope | Delegating access can be done only by the Owner of the account. |
| How-to Guide | The owner of the account can provide delegated access by following these steps: On your computer, open Gmail into an Incognito window. You can't add delegates from the Gmail app. Log in using the account you want to provide delegated access to. In the top right corner of the Gmail interface, click the gear icon to open "Settings", then select "See all settings" to access the full settings menu.  Click the Accounts and Import or Accounts tab.  In the "Grant access to your account" section, click Add another account. If you’re using Gmail through your work or school, your organisation may restrict email delegation. If you don’t see this setting, contact your admin. Enter the email address of the person you want to add.  Click Next Step    Send email to grant access.  The person you added will get an email asking them to confirm. The invitation expires after a week. Note: It may take up to 24 hours for the delegation to start taking effect. |
