## Preparation Steps
1. If you have an iPhone: open the App Store and install Microsoft Authenticator
If you have an Android phone: open the Play Store and install Microsoft Authenticator
2. Head to https://aka.ms/mfasetup
3. Log in using your Visma account.

## Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Open the newly installed Microsoft Authenticator App (you DO NOT need to log to
your account in the application you just installed on your phone)

---

2. Tap on the + icon in the top right corner
3. On the next screen select Work or School account

---

Please make sure you tap on the text and not on the (i)

---

4. Then select Scan QR code and scan the QR code provided in the setup process.
