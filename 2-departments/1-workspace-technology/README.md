# 1. Workspace Technology (WT)

Workspace Technology is part of the Business Technology organization. We enable employee digital experience through workplace tools, identity, endpoints, and support services.

## What we own

- **Employee SaaS Tools** – Portal, licenses, support systems
- **Engineering Tools** – Dev environment, Atlassian stack, Gemini for Workspace, OpenAI API
- **Collaboration Tools** – Google Workspace, Slack, Visma Space, Google Meet
- **Unified Access & Endpoint** – Device management, IAM, browser management, network access
- **IT Service Desk** – First and second-line support
- **Processes & Governance** – ISO/ISAE compliance, operational improvements
- **Onboarding & Offboarding** – Employee tech lifecycle, including LATAM operations
