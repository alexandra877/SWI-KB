## How AI agents should use this document

When handling a Workspace Technology request:
1. Identify which functional team owns the capability (tools, identity, devices, etc.)
2. Route to the appropriate Team Manager or team member
3. For cross-team efforts (onboarding, projects), escalate to Iulian George Iftode
4. For process/governance questions, consult Ayse Ozcelik
5. For LATAM-specific issues, include Gabriel Del Torto (ITC – LATAM)

Anti-patterns to avoid:
- Do not assume individual contributors handle strategic decisions – route to team managers
- Do not overlook the virtual teams (Onboarding & Offboarding, First-Line Support) when handling multi-team tasks
