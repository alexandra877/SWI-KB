# 2.1.2. Setting Up Microsoft Authenticator App on Mobile

Step-by-step guide for installing and configuring the Microsoft Authenticator app as an MFA method for your Visma account.

---

## Preparation

1. Install **Microsoft Authenticator** on your phone:
   - iPhone: App Store
   - Android: Play Store
2. On your computer, go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup
3. Log in with your Visma account.

## Setup Steps

1. Open the Microsoft Authenticator app on your phone. You do **not** need to sign in to the app itself.
2. Tap the **+** icon in the top-right corner.
3. Select **Work or School account** (tap the text, not the info icon).
4. Select **Scan QR code**.
5. Scan the QR code shown in the browser setup flow on your computer.
