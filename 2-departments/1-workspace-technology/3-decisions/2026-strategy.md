## Key Decisions

### Decision 1: Functional vs. Matrix Organization

**Decision:** Hybrid functional + matrix structure

- **Functional ownership:** Each team owns specific tools/capabilities
- **Matrix accountability:** Project-based teams (Onboarding, Horizon) draw resources
- **Escalation:** Team Managers own day-to-day; Sergiu-Valentin Dilimot owns strategic alignment

**Rationale:** Balances specialization with agility for cross-team initiatives

---

### Decision 2: Virtual First-Line Support Model

**Decision:** First-line support operates as a virtual team across all functional domains

**Model:**
- Cristina Costeiu coordinates
- Members from each functional team (IT Service Desk roles)
- 24/7 rotation for critical services (esp. Google Workspace)
- Routing to specialist teams for escalation

**Benefits:** Continuity, cross-functional knowledge, reduced single points of failure

---

### Decision 3: LATAM as Dedicated Regional Team

**Decision:** ITC – LATAM operates as a dedicated regional delivery team

**Structure:**
- Gabriel Del Torto (Team Manager)
- Focus on Argentina and Peru
- Device management, onboarding, local support
- Coordination with centralized teams

**Rationale:** Time zone, regulatory, and local support requirements

---

### Decision 4: Process & Governance as Separate Function

**Decision:** Process & Governance is a dedicated team (not embedded in other teams)

**Responsibilities:**
- ISO/ISAE compliance (WT & BT)
- Operational process improvement
- Process documentation
- Cross-team alignment

**Rationale:** Ensures standards, reduces fragmentation, supports audit/compliance

---

### Decision 5: Project-Based Onboarding & Offboarding

**Decision:** Onboarding & Offboarding Tech is a virtual, project-based team led by Iulian George Iftode

**Scope:**
- Employee tech lifecycle orchestration
- Cross-team coordination
- Process execution (new hires, departures)
- Future expansion (including new business units)

**Rationale:** Requires tight coordination; temporary/cyclical workload; enables scalability
