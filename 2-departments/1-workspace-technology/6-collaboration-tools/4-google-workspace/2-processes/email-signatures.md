# Email Signatures — Google Workspace

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Contact:** vit-d-workspace@visma.com
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Provide consistent, branded email signatures for groups and Organisational Units (OUs) in Visma. The team manages signatures at scale through automation — individual one-off setups are not provided.

---

## Scope

- **In scope:** Group-level or OU-level signature automation
- **Out of scope:** Individual signature customisation (self-service options available below)

---

## Automated Group Signatures

The Workspace team can implement a unified email signature format based on your group or Organisational Unit (OU).

- Signatures are automatically populated from the **Visma Organisation Management (VOM)** system
- Any updates to a user's VOM profile are reflected in their signature automatically
- This service is provided at **no cost**

**To request a group signature:**  
Email **vit-d-workspace@visma.com** — a team member will follow up shortly.

---

## Self-Service: Individual Signature Setup

For individual signature customisation, two options are available:

1. **Google Support guide** — [Set up a Gmail signature](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/8395)
2. **Visma Signature Generator** — a dedicated tool with pre-designed Visma templates for consistent brand identity; custom templates can be requested through the generator if your company requires a unique design

---

## Escalation

| Issue | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Group/OU signature setup or update | Email vit-d-workspace@visma.com |
| VOM profile data not updating in signature | Update your VOM profile; allow 24 hours to sync |
| Custom template request | Use the Visma Signature Generator and follow the custom template request flow |
