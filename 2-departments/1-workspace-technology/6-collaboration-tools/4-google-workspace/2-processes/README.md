# Google Workspace — Processes

SOPs and how-to guides for Google Workspace services managed by the Collaboration Tools team.

## Current Processes

### Email

- `email-signatures.md` — Group/OU email signature setup and self-service options
- `shared-mailbox-delegate-access.md` — Grant delegated access to a Shared Mailbox
- `shared-mailbox-send-as.md` — Send email on behalf of a Shared Mailbox
- `gmail-send-as-alias.md` — Send mail as a Gmail alias

### Google Drive — Shared Drives

- `shared-drive-create.md` — Create a new Shared Drive
- `shared-drive-members.md` — Add or remove Shared Drive members and assign roles
- `shared-drive-find-managers.md` — Find the Manager(s) of a Shared Drive
- `shared-drive-rename.md` — Rename a Shared Drive

### Google Calendar & Meet

- `meeting-rooms-calendar.md` — Book/remove meeting rooms, subscribe to room calendars, Companion Mode

### Troubleshooting

- `troubleshoot-google-docs.md` — What to do when Google Docs is not working
