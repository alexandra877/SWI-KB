# Meeting Rooms in Google Calendar

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Guide employees on how to book, remove, and subscribe to meeting room resources in Google Calendar, and how to use Companion Mode during hybrid meetings.

---

## Key Rules

- Only **Google Workspace admins** in the Workspace team can add new meeting rooms (resources) to Google Calendar
- To request a new resource, create a ticket at **support.visma.com**
- Meeting rooms are called **resources** in Google Calendar
- For events with **fewer than 20 guests**: the organiser and guests receive an email when room details change
- For events with **more than 20 guests**: only the organiser and event creator receive room change notifications

---

## How to Book a Meeting Room

1. Open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com)
2. Create a new event or open an existing one (click **Edit event** for existing events)
3. Click **Rooms** (visible if your account is part of an organisation with rooms configured)
4. Choose from suggested rooms based on your invitees, or search for a specific room
5. Click the room(s) to add them — you can book more than one room per event
6. Click **Save**

---

## How to Remove a Room from an Event

1. Open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com) and open the event
2. Click **Edit event** (top right)
3. In the **Rooms** list, find the room or resource
4. Click **Remove room** next to it
5. Click **Save**

---

## Why a Room May Decline Your Event

| Scenario | Reason / Action |
|----------|-----------------|
| Recurring event | The room must be available for at least half of the occurrences and not blocked more than 8 times — select "Rooms for future events" at the top of the Rooms list |
| Single event | You may need permission — open a ticket at support.visma.com |

---

## How to Subscribe to a Room Calendar

1. Open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com)
2. On the left, next to **Other calendars**, click **+** → **Browse Resources**
3. Click on the building abbreviation you are looking for
4. Tick the checkbox for the resource to subscribe

---

## Companion Mode (Hybrid Meetings)

Use Companion Mode to join a meeting from your laptop while physically in a meeting room, without echo or audio conflict.

### Connect

1. In the meeting invite, click **Join**
2. On the landing page, under **Other joining options**, click **Use Companion mode**
3. Click **Check in** to associate your name with the meeting room — your name will appear in the room's video tile
4. If there is more than one room, select which room you are in

> **Note:** For full audio and video control, join in regular (non-Companion) mode on a separate screen.

### Turn On Your Camera

- Click **Turn on the camera** in the meeting
- If you checked in to a room, the room name appears in your personal tile
- Your tile also appears when you are presenting or under the room name for other Meet users

### Share Your Screen

- Click **Present now** and choose to share your entire screen, a window, or a tab
