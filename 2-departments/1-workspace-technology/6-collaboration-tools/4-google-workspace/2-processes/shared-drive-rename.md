# Rename a Shared Drive

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Audience:** Shared Drive Managers
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Guide Shared Drive Managers through renaming a Shared Drive.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must have the **Manager** role on the Shared Drive

---

## Steps

**Option A — From the left panel:**
1. Locate the drive in the left panel under **Shared drives**
2. Right-click on the drive name and select **Rename**

**Option B — From inside the drive:**
1. Open the Shared Drive
2. In the top-left corner, click on the drive name
3. Edit the name inline and confirm

---

## Notes

- If you are not a Manager and need the drive renamed, contact one of the drive's Managers (see [Find Shared Drive Managers](shared-drive-find-managers.md))
