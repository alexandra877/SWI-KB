**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)

## Purpose

Direct employees to the correct support channel when Google Docs — or any Google Docs feature — is not working.

## Resolution

If Google Docs or any of its features are not working as expected, create a support ticket at **support.visma.com**.

The Google Workspace team will investigate and assist.
