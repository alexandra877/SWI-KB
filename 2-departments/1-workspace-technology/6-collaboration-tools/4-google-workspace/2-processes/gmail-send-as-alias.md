# Send Mail as a Gmail Alias

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Allow a Visma employee to send email from a Gmail alias address using their standard Visma Gmail account.

---

## Steps

1. In your Visma Gmail, click the **gear icon** (top right) → **See all settings**
2. Go to the **Accounts and Import** tab
3. Under **Send mail as:**, click **Add another email address**
4. Enter the alias address you want to send from, then click **Next**
5. Click **Send Verification** — a confirmation email will be sent to the alias address
6. Confirm the verification (click the link or enter the code from the confirmation email)
7. The alias is now available in the **From** field when composing email
