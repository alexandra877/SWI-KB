# Create a Shared Drive in Google

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28
**Source date:** 2024-03-13

---

## Purpose

Guide employees through creating a new Google Shared Drive and managing initial access.

---

## Prerequisites

- Active Visma Google Workspace account
- Permission to create Shared Drives (if the option is grayed out, raise a ticket — see below)

---

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Google Drive** (drive.google.com)
2. In the left panel, right-click on **Shared drives** and select **New Shared Drive**
3. Enter the desired name in the pop-up window and click **Create**
4. At this point, you are the only member of the Shared Drive — add members as needed (see [Shared Drive Members guide](shared-drive-members.md))

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Action |
|-------|--------|
| "New Shared Drive" option is grayed out or missing | Create a ticket at **support.visma.com** to request access |
| Drive creation fails | Contact the Google Workspace team via support.visma.com |
