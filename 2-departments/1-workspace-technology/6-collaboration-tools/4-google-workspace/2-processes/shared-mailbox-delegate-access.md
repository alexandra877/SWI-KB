# Provide Delegated Access to a Shared Mailbox

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Audience:** Owners of Service Accounts / Shared Mailboxes
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Allow a Shared Mailbox owner to grant delegated access to another Visma employee so they can read and send email from that mailbox.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must be the **Owner** of the Shared Mailbox / Service Account
- Steps must be performed from a **desktop browser** (not the Gmail app)
- Use an **Incognito window** to log in to the shared mailbox

---

## Steps

1. On your computer, open **Chrome in an Incognito window**
2. Navigate to **https://mail.google.com** and log in using the **Shared Mailbox** credentials
3. Click the **gear icon** (top right) → **See all settings**
4. Go to the **Accounts and Import** (or **Accounts**) tab
5. In the **Grant access to your account** section, click **Add another account**
6. Enter the email address of the person you want to grant access to
7. Click **Next Step** → **Send email to grant access**
8. The person will receive a confirmation email — they must accept within **one week** (invitation expires after 7 days)

> **Note:** It may take up to **24 hours** for the delegation to take effect after the invitation is accepted.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Action |
|-------|--------|
| "Grant access to your account" section is not visible | Your organisation may restrict email delegation; contact the Google Workspace team |
| Delegation not active after 24 hours | Ask the delegate to check their inbox for the confirmation email; re-send if expired |
