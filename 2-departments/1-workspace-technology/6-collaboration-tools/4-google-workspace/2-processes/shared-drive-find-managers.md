# Find the Manager(s) of a Shared Drive

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Help employees identify who has Manager access to a Shared Drive so they can request to be added or resolve governance issues.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must already be a member of the Shared Drive to view its member list

---

## Steps

1. In your browser, navigate to the Shared Drive you want to inspect
2. In the top-left corner, below the drive name, you will see a number followed by a people icon (e.g. "3 people" or "1 group") — this shows how many members have access
3. Click on that text — a window opens displaying all members and their roles
4. Members with the **Manager** role are able to add new members to the drive

---

## FAQ

**Q: Who can add new members to a shared drive?**  
A: Only members with the **Manager** role.

**Q: I am not a member of the drive — how do I get access?**  
A: Ask a colleague who is already a member to look up the Managers using the steps above, and then have one of the Managers add you.
