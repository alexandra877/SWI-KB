# Send Email on Behalf of a Shared Mailbox

**Owner:** Google Workspace Team (Collaboration Tools)
**Audience:** Users with delegated or owner access to a Shared Mailbox
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Configure Gmail to allow sending email on behalf of a Shared Mailbox from your personal Visma inbox.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must have access to the Shared Mailbox (either as owner or delegated access)
- Access to both your personal Gmail and the Shared Mailbox inbox

---

## Steps

1. In your **personal Gmail**, click the **gear icon** (top right) → **See all settings**
2. Go to the **Accounts and Import** tab
3. Under **Send mail as:**, click **Add another email address**
4. A new window opens — enter a display name and the **Shared Mailbox email address**, then click **Next Step**
5. Click **Send Verification**
6. In the Shared Mailbox's inbox, you will receive an email with a **verification code** and a confirmation link
7. Return to your personal Gmail and either:
   - Click the link in the confirmation email (if you clicked through), or
   - Enter the verification code you received
8. The setup is complete — you can now select the Shared Mailbox address in the **From** field when composing email
