# Set Up Work Profile on Android Phones

**Owner:** Unified Access & Endpoint (Device Management)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Guide employees through enrolling their Android device into Visma's mobile device management (MDM) and creating a work profile that keeps personal and work apps separate.

---

## Prerequisites

- A Visma Google Workspace account (firstname.lastname@visma.com)
- Android device (personal or company-owned)

---

## Step 1: Set Up Your Device

**Personal device:**

1. If the device is new or factory-reset: start it and follow prompts to the Google sign-in screen
2. If the device is already in use: go to **Settings** → **Accounts** → **Add account** → **Google**
3. Enter your **Visma Google Workspace email** (firstname.lastname@visma.com) and tap **Next**
4. Enter your password and tap **Next**
5. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy — tap **I agree**
6. If prompted, tap **More** → **Next** → **Next**, then choose:
   - **Use for work only**, or
   - **Use for work & personal**
7. If asked to create a screen lock, tap **Next** and follow the steps — you are done
8. If asked to install a device policy app, continue to Step 2

**Company-owned device:** Follow the same steps; you will likely be directed to Step 2.

---

## Step 2: Install the Android Device Policy App

> **Note:** If a screen appears asking you to scan or enter a code from an admin, exit that screen and manually install/update the **Android Device Policy** app first.

1. At the prompt, tap **Install**
2. Tap **Install** again to confirm
3. Tap **Accept & continue** to create a work profile on the device
4. Tap **Next** and follow prompts to complete work profile setup
5. If prompted, tap **Start** and follow steps to set a screen lock
6. Tap **Install** → **Next** to install work apps
7. Tap **Done**

---

## Step 3: Start Using Work Apps

- Work apps are marked with a **briefcase icon**
- To access all work apps: swipe up → tap **Work**
- To find and install additional work apps: go to **managed Google Play**

> Depending on your organisation's settings, some apps may not be installable. Contact your administrator for help.
