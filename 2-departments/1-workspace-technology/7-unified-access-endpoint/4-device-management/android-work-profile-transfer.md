# Transfer Android Work Profile to a New Phone

**Owner:** Unified Access & Endpoint (Device Management)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Clarify the process when an employee gets a new Android phone and needs to move their work profile.

---

## Important Note

**Work profiles cannot be transferred between devices.** If you have a new phone, you must set up the work profile from scratch on the new device.

---

## Action Required

Follow the [Set Up Work Profile on Android Phones](android-work-profile-setup.md) guide to enrol your new device from the beginning.
