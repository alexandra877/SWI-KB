# Remove Work Profile from Android Device

**Owner:** Unified Access & Endpoint (Device Management)
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Guide employees or IT staff through removing a work profile from an Android device — for example when troubleshooting, or when an employee leaves the company and takes the device with them.

---

## Prerequisites

- You must **own the device** to delete the work profile yourself
- Have your device's **serial number or IMEI** ready if requesting a remote wipe

---

## Steps (Self-Service)

> Note: exact steps may vary slightly depending on the Android device model.

1. Go to **Settings** → **Accounts**
2. Tap **Remove work profile**
3. Tap **Delete** to confirm — all apps and data within the work profile will be deleted
4. Verify that the **Device Policy** app is uninstalled and no longer present on the device

---

## Remote Wipe (IT-Managed)

If self-service removal is not working, contact IT support and provide your device's **serial number or IMEI** to request a remote wipe.

> **Important — know your setup before requesting a remote wipe:**

| Setup | Wipe result |
|-------|-------------|
| Private email set as primary account, Visma as secondary | Only the work profile is deleted; personal data stays intact |
| Visma account set as primary account | Entire phone is reset to factory settings — **all data including personal data is lost** |

**In both cases, create a backup of any data you want to keep before requesting a wipe.**
