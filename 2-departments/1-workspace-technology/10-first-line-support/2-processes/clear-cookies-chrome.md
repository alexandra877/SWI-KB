# Clear Cookies and Cache in Google Chrome

**Owner:** First-Line Support
**Audience:** Employees and IT Service Desk staff
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Resolve browser-related issues (login loops, stale sessions, rendering problems) by clearing cookies and cached files in Google Chrome.

---

## Steps

1. Open **Google Chrome**
2. Click the **three vertical dots** (top right) → **Delete browsing data...**
   - Alternatively, press **Ctrl + Shift + Del** on your keyboard
3. Set the **Time range** to **All time**
4. Select the items to remove:
   - **Cookies and other site data**
   - **Cached images and files**
5. Click **Clear data**

---

## Notes

- Clearing cookies will sign you out of websites — have your passwords ready
- You can clear just cached images/files if you want to preserve login sessions
