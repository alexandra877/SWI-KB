# Assist Employees with 2FA — Google Backup Codes

**Owner:** First-Line Support / Google Workspace Team
**Audience:** IT support staff with a GOO (Google Workspace Admin) account or OU admin access
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Allow IT support staff to generate Google 2FA backup codes on behalf of an employee who is locked out or unable to use their primary 2FA method.

---

## Prerequisites

- You or a colleague must have a **GOO account** (Google Workspace admin account), or be an **OU admin** for the relevant company

---

## Steps

1. Log in to [admin.google.com](https://admin.google.com) with your GOO account and password
2. In the search bar, type the **email address or name** of the user (works for internal accounts and CPA accounts)
3. Open the user and go to the **Security** tab
4. Scroll down to **2-step verification** and click the box for **Get backup verification codes**
5. Provide the employee with **two** of the 8-digit backup codes shown
6. If no backup codes are available, click **Generate new ones**

---

## Employee Instructions — Using a Backup Code

The employee should follow these steps:

1. Go to [mail.google.com](https://mail.google.com) and start sign-in
2. When prompted for 2FA, click **Try another way**
3. Click **Enter one of your 8-digit backup codes**
4. Enter one of the unused backup codes provided

---

## Preventing Future Lockouts

Advise the employee to activate 2FA properly:
- Click **Enroll Now** when prompted, or
- Visit [myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security) to set up 2FA
