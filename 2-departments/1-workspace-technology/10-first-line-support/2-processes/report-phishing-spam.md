# Report Phishing or Spam Email

**Owner:** First-Line Support / Security Team
**Audience:** All employees and IT Service Desk staff
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-28

---

## Purpose

Guide employees through correctly reporting phishing or spam emails received in Gmail, ensuring the security team is notified of potential threats.

---

## Report a Phishing Email

> **Important:** Do not click any links in a suspected phishing email.

1. Open the suspected phishing email in Gmail — do not click any links
2. Click the **three dots** (more options) in the upper-right corner of the email
3. Select **Report phishing**
4. A ticket is automatically sent to the **Visma Security team**, who will investigate and follow up with you

---

## Report a Spam Email

1. Open the spam email in Gmail — do not click any links
2. Click the **three dots** (more options) in the upper-right corner of the email
3. Select **Report spam**
4. The email moves to your Spam folder

---

## Key Difference

| Type | Who handles it | What happens |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Phishing | Visma Security team | Ticket created; team investigates |
| Spam | Gmail / automated | Email moved to Spam folder |
