# 10.1. First-Line Support — Overview

**Lead:** Cristina Costeiu
**Type:** Virtual operational team
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-22

---

## Purpose

Handle employee IT requests at first contact across all Workspace Technology domains — triaging, resolving, or escalating issues to the appropriate functional team.

---

## Coverage Areas

| Domain | Escalation Team |
|--------|----------------|
| Employee SaaS Tools | Employee SaaS Tools team |
| Engineering Tools | Engineering Tools team |
| Collaboration Tools (Google Workspace, Slack) | Collaboration Tools team |
| Unified Access & Endpoint (devices, IAM, network) | Unified Access & Endpoint team |
| LATAM | ITC – LATAM team |

---

## Team Composition

First-Line Support is a virtual team. IT Service Desk members from across all functional teams contribute to first-line coverage.

| Name | Role |
|------|------|
| Cristina Costeiu | Team Lead |

---

## Escalation Model

1. Employee raises a ticket via support.visma.com or the Employee Portal
2. First-Line Support triages and attempts resolution
3. If unresolved, escalates to the owning functional team with context and steps already taken
4. Functional team resolves and closes with First-Line Support in the loop

---

## For agents

When handling a support question that has not been resolved, route to the functional team that owns the relevant domain. First-Line Support is the intake layer, not the resolver for complex issues.
