# 9.1. Onboarding & Offboarding Tech Team — Overview

**Lead:** Iulian George Iftode
**Type:** Cross-functional project team
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-22

---

## Purpose

Coordinate the end-to-end technical lifecycle of employees joining and leaving Visma — from account creation and device provisioning to access revocation and equipment return — drawing from all WT functional teams.

---

## Scope

- New joiner technical setup (accounts, devices, access, tools)
- Leaver technical teardown (access revocation, device retrieval, data handling)
- Cross-team coordination to ensure nothing falls through the gaps
- LATAM onboarding/offboarding coordination
