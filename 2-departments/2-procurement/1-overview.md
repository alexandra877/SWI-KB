## Organizational Structure

Procurement is organized around major vendor and contract categories:

### Vendor Categories

- **Software & Cloud** – SaaS platforms, development tools, cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
- **Security & Compliance** – Security tools, access management, compliance platforms
- **IT Equipment & Infrastructure** – Hardware, servers, data center services
- **Facilities & Operations** – Office services, facility management, HR platforms
- **Finance & Banking** – Payment services, banking integrations
- **Professional Services** – Consulting, implementation, specialized services
