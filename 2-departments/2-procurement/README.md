# 2. Procurement & Vendor Management

Procurement & Vendor Management owns contract lifecycle, vendor relationships, and SaaS/software cost optimization for Visma.

## What we own

- **Contract management** – Active contracts, renewals, renegotiations
- **Vendor relationships** – Onboarding, performance, compliance
- **Procurement processes** – Vendor selection, approval workflows, compliance
- **Cost optimization** – SaaS rationalization, license management, contract analytics
- **Compliance & governance** – Finance policies, security/legal review, audit trail

## Contents

- `1-overview.md` — Team structure, responsibilities, procurement specialists
- `2-processes/` — Procurement workflows, contract approval, vendor onboarding
- `3-decisions/` — Strategic vendor decisions, contract negotiations
- `contracts-registry.md` — Active contracts (113), vendors, dates, owners

## For agents

Load this department folder when:
- Researching vendor options or active contracts
- Understanding procurement processes and approval workflows
- Identifying cost optimization opportunities
- Managing vendor relationships or compliance
- Finding contract details, end dates, or procurement contacts

Always consult `contracts-registry.md` for the current contract status.
