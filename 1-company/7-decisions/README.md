# 1.6. Decisions

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) for company-level decisions.

## What Goes Here

Decisions that affect the entire organisation — structure, strategy, major policies, significant pivots. Department and product-specific decisions belong in their respective layers.

## Format

Each decision is a numbered file: `adr-001-title.md`

Use this template:

```markdown
## ADR-NNN: Title

**Date:** DD/MM/YYYY
**Status:** Accepted
**Authors:** names

### Context
Why this decision was needed.

### Decision
What was decided.

### Rationale
Why this option was chosen over alternatives.

### Consequences
What changes as a result.
```
