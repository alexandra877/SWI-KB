# 4.1.2. AI Championship Program — Plan

---

## Change Management Approach

The programme uses **Kotter's 8-Step Change Management Model**, with people — not technology — at the centre.

| Step | Focus |
|---|---|
| 1 | Create a Sense of Urgency |
| 2 | Build a Guiding Coalition |
| 3 | Form a Strategic Vision |
| 4 | Enlist a Volunteer Army (Communicate the Vision) |
| 5 | Enable Action by Removing Barriers / Measure Baseline |
| 6 | Generate Short-Term Wins |
| 7 | Sustain Acceleration |
| 8 | Institute Change (Anchor in Culture) |

Steps 1–4 make the plan scalable. Step 4 ("train the trainer") is where AI Mentors multiply impact across teams.
